About the Campaign

The 'Say No, Say Yes' campaign is an initiative by the National Health Fellows (NHF) in Bakassi, Nigeria, dedicated to combating drug abuse among Nigerian youth through education, awareness, and community engagement.

Our mission is to empower young Nigerians with knowledge and support systems that enable them to make informed decisions about drugs and substance abuse. By fostering a community that prioritizes health and wellbeing, we aim to create a drug-free future for Nigerian youth.
